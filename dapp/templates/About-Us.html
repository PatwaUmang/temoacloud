About The TEMOA Project

Tools for Energy Model Optimization and Assessment (Temoa) is an open source modeling framework for conducting energy system analysis. The core component of Temoa is a technology explicit energy economy optimization model. It's similar in design to the MARKAL / TIMES model generators, OSeMOSYS, and MESSAGE. Read More
